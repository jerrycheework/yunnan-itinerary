🚄 Transportation Guide

Complete guide to getting around Yunnan Province

Kunming → Dali → Lijiang → Shangri-La → Kunming

🚄
High-Speed Rail (CRH)
Kunming → Dali
⏱️ 2 hours 💰 ¥145-220
Distance: 300 km
Frequency: Every 30-60 minutes
Shangri-La → Kunming
⏱️ 4-5 hours 💰 ¥280-450
Distance: 650 km
Limited daily departures

📱 How to Book

Online: 12306.cn (Chinese), Trip.com (English)

Stations: Ticket counters with passport

Apps: Railway 12306, Ctrip

Hotels: Most hotels can book for you

💡 Pro Tips

  • Book 30 days in advance for best prices
  • Arrive 30 minutes early for security
  • Bring passport for ticket purchase and boarding
  • First class worth it for longer journeys
🚌
Regional Buses
Dali → Lijiang
⏱️ 2.5-3 hours 💰 ¥65-85
Mountain highway route
Every 30 minutes (7 AM - 7 PM)
Lijiang → Shangri-La
⏱️ 3.5-4 hours 💰 ¥75-95
Scenic mountain route
6-8 departures daily

🎫 Bus Stations

Dali: Dali Bus Station (大理客运站)

Lijiang: Lijiang Bus Terminal (丽江客运站)

Tickets: Buy at station or online

💡 Bus Travel Tips

  • Bring motion sickness medicine for mountain roads
  • Buses can be cold - bring warm clothes
  • Rest stops every 2 hours
  • Keep ticket until destination
🚗
Private Cars & Tours

Scenic photography stops

📞 How to Arrange

Hotel Concierge: Most reliable option

Local Tour Agencies: In old towns

Online: Klook, GetYourGuide

DiDi: For city transportation

💡 Private Transport Tips

  • Negotiate price beforehand
  • Ensure driver speaks some English
  • Confirm all stops in itinerary
  • Get driver's WeChat for communication

🛂 Airport Procedures

Arrival: Immigration → Baggage → Customs → Exit

Departure: Check-in → Security → Immigration → Gate

Time: Arrive 2-3 hours before international flights

🏔️ High Altitude Tips

  • Buy oxygen canisters (¥50-80)
  • Go slowly, rest frequently
  • Dress warmly - temperature drops rapidly
  • Book early, limited daily capacity

📱 Essential Apps

DiDi: Chinese Uber (need Chinese number)

Baidu Maps: Best for navigation in China

Metro apps: For each city's metro system

🚕 Taxi Tips

  • Have destination written in Chinese
  • Insist on using meter
  • Keep business card of hotel
  • Download translate app for communication
