🌤️ Weather & Climate Information

Climate Overview

Yunnan Province: Subtropical highland climate with mild temperatures year-round

Best Travel Time: March-May and September-November

Dry Season: November-April (less rain, clearer skies)

Wet Season: May-October (more rain, lush landscapes)

Kunming (1,892m)

Temperature: 15-25°C (59-77°F)

Known as: "City of Eternal Spring"

Pack: Light layers, light jacket

Dali (1,976m)

Temperature: 12-22°C (54-72°F)

Weather: Mild, breezy by the lake

Pack: Windbreaker, comfortable walking shoes

Lijiang (2,418m)

Temperature: 8-20°C (46-68°F)

Weather: Cool, large day-night temperature difference

Pack: Warm jacket, layers

Shangri-La (3,459m)

Temperature: -5-15°C (23-59°F)

Weather: Cold, high altitude

Pack: Winter clothes, warm boots

🚄 Transportation Guide

High-Speed Trains

Kunming-Dali: 2 hours

Shangri-La-Kunming: 4-5 hours

Booking: 12306.cn (Chinese) or through hotels

Tip: Book in advance during peak season

Local Transportation

DiDi: Chinese Uber equivalent (download app)

Taxis: Show destination in Chinese characters

Buses: Very cheap but language barrier

Tour buses: Available for major attractions

Airport Transfers

Kunming Airport: Metro Line 6 to city center

Cost: ¥10-15 for metro, ¥80-120 for taxi

Time: 45-60 minutes to city center

English: Limited, have address in Chinese

💰 Money & Costs

Payment Methods

WeChat Pay: Most common (need Chinese bank account)

Alipay: Alternative mobile payment

Cash: Still widely accepted

Credit Cards: Limited acceptance, mainly hotels

Estimated Daily Costs (per person)

Budget: ¥200-400 ($30-60)

Mid-range: ¥400-800 ($60-120)

Luxury: ¥800+ ($120+)

Attractions: ¥50-200 entrance fees

Tipping Guide

Restaurants: Not expected, but appreciated

Hotels: ¥10-20 for housekeeping

Tour guides: ¥50-100 per day

Drivers: ¥30-50 per day

📶 Internet & Communication

Internet Access

WiFi: Available in hotels, cafes, attractions

SIM Cards: China Mobile, China Unicom at airport

VPN: Required for Google, Facebook, WhatsApp

WeChat: Essential app for communication

Translation Apps

Google Translate: Camera translation (need VPN)

Baidu Translate: Works without VPN

Pleco: Excellent Chinese dictionary

Microsoft Translator: Offline capabilities
