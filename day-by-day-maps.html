📍 24.7797° N, 103.2672° E

UNESCO Site: Karst limestone formations, allow 4-5 hours for visit
⛰️ Western Hills (Xishan)
📍 24.9537° N, 102.6280° E

Day 5: Lijiang Highlights

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain and cultural shows

🏔️ Jade Dragon Snow Mountain
📍 27.1356° N, 100.2075° E
Altitude: Cable car to 4680m - bring warm clothes!
Tip: Buy oxygen canisters, go early to avoid crowds
💙 Blue Moon Valley
📍 27.1167° N, 100.2167° E
Sacred Lakes: Crystal blue glacier-fed pools
Photography: Stunning turquoise water reflections
🎭 Impression Lijiang Theater
📍 27.1300° N, 100.2100° E
Show Times: Usually 7:30 PM and 9:00 PM
Unique: Outdoor performance with mountain backdrop
🏛️ Lijiang Old Town
📍 26.8721° N, 100.2269° E
UNESCO Site: Naxi minority culture
Evening: Beautiful when lit up at night

Wetlands, ancient towns, and evening entertainment

🦅 Lashihai Wetland
📍 26.9342° N, 100.1667° E
Activities: Birdwatching, horseback riding on Tea Horse Road
Best Season: Winter for migratory birds
🐉 Black Dragon Pool Park

Cultural: Dongba Culture Museum inside
🏘️ Shuhe Ancient Town

Day 7: Journey to Shangri-La

Scenic drive with Yangtze River and Tiger Leaping Gorge

🌊 First Bend of Yangtze River
📍 26.8667° N, 99.4167° E
Scenic Stop: Famous U-turn of China's longest river
Photo Op: Best views from observation platform
🏔️ Tiger Leaping Gorge
📍 27.1894° N, 100.1064° E
World Famous: One of world's deepest gorges
Hiking: Upper trail accessible, lower trail for experienced hikers
🏛️ Dukezong Ancient Town
📍 27.8269° N, 99.7064° E
Tibetan Culture: 1300-year-old town
Prayer Wheel: World's largest, requires 3+ people to turn
🏯 Songzanlin Monastery
📍 27.8508° N, 99.7128° E
Little Potala: Largest Tibetan monastery in Yunnan
Visit Time: Early morning for monk prayers (7-8 AM)

🌾 Napa Lake Grassland
📍 27.8333° N, 99.6167° E
Seasonal Lake: Grassland in dry season, lake in wet season
Activities: Horseback riding, Black-necked crane watching
🎭 Tibetan Cultural Experience
📍 27.8200° N, 99.7000° E
Traditional Banquet: Yak meat, barley wine, butter tea
Entertainment: Traditional Tibetan song and dance performances
